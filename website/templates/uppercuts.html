The Lead Uppercut - 5

A punch thrown with the lead hand at nearly a vertical angle toward the opponent’s chin. Invented by Samuel Elias aka 'Dutch Sam' in the late 18th century.
Notable Users: Ricardo Lopez, Gervonta 'Tank' Davis, 'Iron' Mike Tyson

The Rear Uppercut - 6

A punch thrown with the rear hand at a nearly vertical angle toward the opponent’s chin
Notable Users: Jermall Charlo, Lennox Lewis, Riddick Bowe

Corkscrew Punch

A very powerful straight where the power is generated by the torque of twisting the fist such that the palm faces outward.
Notable Users: Charles Kid McCoy, Wladamir Klitschko, Anthony Joshua

The Cross

Distinct from the straight in that the elbow is slightly bent and the punch is thrown around and over the opponent’s jab across the body. It doesn’t necessarily have to be thrown over the jab to be considered a cross, the mechanics are slightly different in that the punch does not come in a straight line but a very small arc and across from the right side to the left.
Notable Users: Muhammad Ali, Floyd Mayweather, Archie Moore

Feint/Fake Cross

A tool used to disorient your opponent or create an opening, executed by only partially extending the rear hand then retracting it.
Notable Users: Muhammad Ali, Saul 'Canelo' Alvarez, Tyson Fury

Looping Punch

This punch resembles a hook but has a longer range and a much wider arc than all other variants, often goes around the guard and hits the side of the temple or on the ear.
Notable Users: Erislandy Lara, Terrence Crawford, Larry Holmes

Overcut/Overhand Right/Left

A more exaggerated form of the cross, the arc of the punch flies over the head of the boxer throwing the punch and crashes down on the opponent’s cheek, temple or chin. Executed generally during or after a drop step or transitioning from an upright stance to a full crouch.
Notable Users: Shawn Porter, Joe Louis, Jack Dempsey

Short Straight

Less powerful than the other variants the short straight is thrown from a closer range in some cases room is provided for the punch during the punch by pulling the upper body back as you throw the punch. Usually thrown when the back is on the ropes.
Notable Users: James Toney, Lennox Lewis, Floyd Mayweather

Straight to the body

Similar to the straight but requires more of a lunging motion to reach the body and in some cases loops around the guard.
Notable Users: Floyd Mayweather, Roy Jones Jr., Marcos Maidana

Underhand Straight

Generally used by shorter fighters but can be used when in a crouched position, this punch is similar to the 45-degree jab where the punch comes up from a low angle.
Notable Users: Guillermo Rigondieux, Manny Pacquiao
